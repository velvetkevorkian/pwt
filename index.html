What is plain white tees?

Plain White Tees is an event designed to get you messy. Bring along a white shirt you don't mind getting wrecked and some colour pens - and prepare to make some new friends. If you don't happen to have a shirt you don't mind saying goodbye to, our PR team have a limited number of branded shirts for sale for only £3! During the event we encourage people to draw all over each other, friends and strangers alike. The event is to raise funds for our degree show - the more fun you have, the better our display!

Bargain Prices

The event is at Dundee University Students Union, with some of the cheapest entry prices and drinks in town! It's SAAS day so there is no excuse not to come out and spend your hard earned loan. Drinks start from just 99p. Advance tickets are just £3 in advance, available from the PR team, the Premier union shop, and online, but they're £4 on the door so make sure you buy in advance. That extra pound is one more drink on the night and who can say no to just one more drink?

Pens Provided

On the night, you'll be needing pens to play along - so on the door we thought we'll give you one. It's one pen per ticket so if you want a little more range for your artistic tendencies we'd suggest bringing some more to share among your friends. Felt tip pens work best on the shirts (and cause minimal pain for the new friend you're drawing on). Avoid sharpies, as they tend to be more permanent than the fun of the night.

See you there!
